The Problem

Optimisation for the Future Metropolis

The power of sensors and the cloud is harnessed to build this comparatively low cost optimization solution. In an ecologically sensitive world as ours it is for the best of humanity that everyday processes have to be optimised.

The Solution

Ultrasonic Sensor modules make up the sensory division of the system. These modules are connected to local dustbins which then collects data on the amount of garbage in the bin and updates it to the cloud in real time. This raw data is then analysed by a central system which then calculates and maps out the optimal route for garbage collection around the neighborhood thus saving both time, fuel and the smell.

Aftermath

When the fundamentals are right, the rest works accordingly. As more and more people migrate into urban regions, it is imperative that we are able to use the influx of human potential to the best and for that cities should be equipped to handle the increased population. Centralised garbage collection system is envisioned as a step in that direction. A step towards a sustainable and developed society.
